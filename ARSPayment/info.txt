-----------Installation------------

1. Copy the contents of this package, excluding info.txt to your opencart installation keeping the folder structure.
2. Login to the Open Cart admin section and go to Extensions > Payments
3. Find ARS_Payment in the list of extensions
4. Click "Install" and then "Edit" the payment module settings
5. Set your Token and Save
